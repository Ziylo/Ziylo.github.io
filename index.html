An Introduction to Cantiamo Singers

Cantiamo was formed in 2017 as a new group of (currently 15) singers based in North Somerset. Membership is by invitation and rehearsals take place in All Saints’ Church, Wrington which is the ‘home’ of the Singers.

The Singers draw much on the repertoire of unaccompanied Renaissance music but also enjoy exploring more recent writing. They pay much attention to musicality, sonority, and clarity of diction.

Cantiamo has held several concerts since resuming rehearsals in September 2021, including one with Barry Rose, the former organist of Guildford, St Paul's, and St Alban's Cathedrals, as a guest recitalist.

Under the direction of Jeremy Martin, Cantiamo Singers continue to plan and hold concerts, as well as performing in various services at Wrington.

Who are we?

Cantiamo Singers are a friendly group of singers, directed by Jeremy Martin. We perform both secular and sacred music from the Renaissance to the 21st century.
